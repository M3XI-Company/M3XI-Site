Vision

To build a future where software and robotics seamlessly expand human capability, creativity, and opportunity in everyday life.

M3XI envisions a world where intelligent software and domestic robotics become natural parts of daily life—empowering individuals, businesses, and creators while generating the data that fuels the next generation of artificial intelligence.

Mission

To create technology that unlocks opportunity, accelerates innovation, and normalizes robotics within the home.

M3XI builds software platforms that give overlooked businesses access to opportunity and visibility; domestic robotics systems that bring practical automation into everyday households; and high-quality training datasets generated from real applications to power the next wave of AI development.
