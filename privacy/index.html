Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how M3XI and StartUp ("we", "us", "our") collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use the StartUp mobile application and M3XI website. By using our services, you agree to the terms described here.

1. Who We Are

M3XI is a technology company based in England and Wales. StartUp is a mobile application developed and operated by M3XI. For data protection purposes, M3XI is the controller of your personal data.

Contact: support@m3xi.com

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly and information generated through your use of our services:

  • Account information: Name, email address, LinkedIn profile data (when signing in with LinkedIn).
  • Profile data: Business descriptions, ideas, posts, and content you publish to the feed.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, interactions with posts and other users.
  • Payment data: Processed securely via Stripe. We do not store card details.
  • Device data: Device type, operating system, and app version for troubleshooting.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the StartUp platform and M3XI services.
  • To match users with relevant investors and business opportunities via the feed algorithm.
  • To generate business analysis and valuation reports on request.
  • To process payments for subscriptions and premium features.
  • To send service notifications (not marketing, unless you opt in).
  • To ensure platform safety and enforce our Terms and Conditions.

4. LinkedIn Sign-In

When you sign in with LinkedIn, we receive your public profile information (name, photo, email) via LinkedIn's OAuth service. We use this solely for account creation and identity verification. We do not post to LinkedIn on your behalf.

5. Your Ideas and Content

You retain full ownership of any ideas, business plans, or content you post on StartUp. We do not claim ownership of your intellectual property. Other users are contractually prohibited from misappropriating your ideas under our Terms and Conditions.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Supabase: Our backend infrastructure provider (data hosting and authentication).
  • Stripe: Payment processing (card data never touches our servers).
  • LinkedIn: Only for OAuth authentication.
  • Legal authorities: When required by law or to protect safety.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, personal data is removed within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

8. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Restrict or object to processing.
  • Data portability.

To exercise any of these rights, contact support@m3xi.com.

9. Cookies

The StartUp app does not use browser cookies. Our website uses minimal session cookies required for functionality. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

10. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), Row Level Security on our database, and secure OAuth flows. No system is 100% secure — please use a strong, unique password and report any concerns to support@m3xi.com.

11. Children

StartUp is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has registered, please contact us immediately.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of significant changes via the app or email.

13. Contact

For all privacy-related enquiries: support@m3xi.com

For StartUp app support: startup@m3xi.com
